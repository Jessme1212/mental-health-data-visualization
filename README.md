# mental-health-data-visualization
Data visualization exploring mental health provider access and suicide rates across U.S. states.

Mental Health Access Gap in the United States

This project explores the relationship between mental health provider availability and suicide rates across U.S. states.

Key Insights
-States with lower provider availability often show higher suicide rates
-Regional disparities highlight gaps in access to care
-The relationship is complex and influenced by additional factors

Tools Used
-Power BI

Data visualization techniques
-Public health datasets (CDC, HRSA, U.S. Census)

Purpose
-To highlight disparities in mental health access and support data-driven decision-making in healthcare.
